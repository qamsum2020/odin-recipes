Karachi Special Biryani

This is a special Biryani which is very famous all over Pakistan.

Karachi Biryani is eaten by people all over the world.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Chicken
  • Masala
  • Cooking Oil

Steps

  1. Boil the rice
  2. Fry chicken
  3. Add chicken to rice
  4. Serve with raita
